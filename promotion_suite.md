Hey everyone!

I've always struggled with keeping track of all the characters, environments, and camera movements when building complex prompts for Midjourney and video models like Sora. 

So, I built a free tool called **Mapping AI Prompt**. It lets you build your prompt using visual blocks, adds "Magic" technical keywords with one click, and even translates from your native language to high-end English.

You can also download a "Recipe Card" of your prompt to share or save.

Check it out here: [https://mappingaiprompt.vercel.app/](https://mappingaiprompt.vercel.app/)

I'd love to hear your feedback or if there are any specific "Magic Keywords" you'd like me to add!

## 💡 Tips untuk Promosi:
1. **Lampirkan Gambar**: Gunakan file `og-image.png` dari folder `public` atau ambil screenshot aplikasi saat digunakan agar postingan lebih menarik.
2. **Balas Komentar**: Jika ada yang bertanya di Reddit, pastikan untuk menjawab. Ini akan meningkatkan visibilitas postingan Anda.
3. **Pilih Waktu yang Tepat**: Posting di hari kerja (Selasa-Kamis) sekitar jam 7-9 malam WIB (pagi waktu US) biasanya mendapatkan lebih banyak penayangan di Reddit.
